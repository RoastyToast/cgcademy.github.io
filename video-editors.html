What file formats do you support?
We currently support video and image files. Audio and PDF support is coming soon!
Do external reviewers have to be a part of my team?
Nope! External reviewers can provide feedback on your files without being a project member.
Is there a limit to the number of review links you can create?
Review links are free and unlimited!
Do you need an account to upload media files through the special upload links?
No login or account is required by default to upload files. You can enforce it if you want during upload link creation process though.
